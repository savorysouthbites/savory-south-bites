Our Story

The Taste of Kerala,
Crafted with Love

Savory South Bites was born from a simple desire — to bring the true, unapologetic flavours of Kerala to the Netherlands. Every dish follows traditional family recipes passed down through generations, prepared with the same care and soul as a home-cooked meal.

From the iconic Thalassery Dum Biryani — fragrant Kaima rice sealed in a dum pot with whole spices — to slow-simmered fish curries in earthen pots, every bite tells the story of God's Own Country.
